Check out some of the best that Bristol has to offer - whatever your budget!

The Beehive Café

Indulge your sweet tooth with a sweet treat! With super-sized milkshakes and incredible toppings, you know that you'll get a bang for your buck.

Pho

Pho, with its distinct focus on the eponymous Vietnamese noodle broth, is a reliable winter-warmer and refreshing summer choice. Vegan and gluten-free options are plentiful and the servings are generous - it's hard to be discrete about this spot!

Pinkman's

Hearty breakfasts. Hand-made pizzas. Rich desserts. Daring coktails. Bread baked on-location. Pinkman's is an all-day treat! The readily-available vegan and gluten-free options mean that everyone will leave feeling tickled-pink.

Chilli Daddy

Chilli Daddy is a chinese restaurant selling sze-chuan style food. Spice levels go from levels 0-5 so love it or hate it, there is enough sze-chuan food for everyone!

Mission Burrito

Mission Burrito sells cheap, student-friendly burritos in the middle of Park Street! Vegan to carnivorous, they've got it all. And, if you grab a loyalty card, you'll be scoffing your 10th burrito for free.

Piri-Piri

Piri-piri is halal and delicious! Need a quick, tasty meal? Come here and kill two birds with one stone.

Swoon

What's in a name? In Swoon's case, there's a lot of truth. With decadent hot chocolates, suave gelatos and extraordinary sorbets, the award-winning gelateria has both vegans and lactophiles falling over themselves.

Atomic Burger

This establishment takes on the humble burger and fries and transforms them into something out of this world! If you want a dining experience that will take you to infinity and beyond then look no further than Atomic Burger.

The Ivy

Sometimes a picture says a thousand words. Would recommend visting when your parents/generous benefactors are in town.

Fresh Takeaway

Fresh is famously known for feeding High Kingsdown residents and those who frequent the ASS (Arts and Social Sciences) library.

Wok to Walk

Wok to Walk is located at the bottom of Park Street and is the simplest chinese takeaway place around - plus it boasts a 20% student discount before 11pm!

Beerd

Don't be discouraged by the hipster decor and the high quality food - Beerd's lunchtime deals are very budget-friendly. If you're feeling cheeky, you can also nab yourself a craft beer!

Yakinori

In truth, Yakinori is a bit of an enigma, but it is beautiful. From the mildly sapphic mural to the gorgeous food and lovely staff to boot, a dining experience here will leave you smiling in that 'I'm not sure what I just experienced but it was fun,' kind of way! Pro tip: you can halve the price of your (very delicious) meal by opting for the takeout bento box option!

Nomu

If you don't eat at Nomu at least once then why even bother coming to Bristol? The food is phenomenal - punchy, flavourful and delicate where it needs to be. The decor and the playlist create an ambience that oozes chill-yet-sophisticated charm.

Urban Tandoor

Voted one of the best curry houses in the country by TripAdvisor and bestowed with the GoodFood Award in 2015, Urban Tandoor is a national force. You really need to go.

Pepenero

Did someone say delicious, halal AND vegan?

Bento Boss

Eat like a BOSS at this beautiful elevated restaurant. The food is refreshing, extremely pleasant for the palate and, best of all, halal!

1847

This restaurant is plant-based heaven. From vegetarian versions of popular meat-based foods to wildly innovative vegan creations, there is something to please every type of palate.

Eat a Pitta

Located right opposite Wills Memorial Building, it offers falafel for just £2.50. You don't have to venture far from your books to indulge in a healthy, filling and (optionally) vegan meal.

Dragon Fountain

Yet more proof that delicious food doesn't have to break the bank! Dragon Fountain's 30 year heritage of great-quality takeout means that your late-night cravings will always be well-satisfied.

Vincenzo's Pizza House

Conveniently located on Park Street and with extremely generous portion sizes for their pizza and pasta dishes, Vinceno's brings a slice of Italy to Bristol.

Mezze Palace

You will find few places with as much value for money - £5 lunches in a cozy city centre restaurant? You might be wondering what the catch is, but we can't find one! It's safe to say that at Mezze Palace, you'll eat like royalty.

Diamond Kebab

Halal, open at inordinate times of night and a reliable source of cheesy chips. Kebabs are the de facto national food and Diamond Kebab knows how to fly the flag.

Taka Taka

Taka Taka is the restaurant equivalent of your most reliable, cheerful, kind friend. They are there for you at all hours and they're always happy to snap you up a quick meal when you're a little tipsy and need something to ground you. You visit this friend and you always leave feeling better. It's not a mistake that their rolls are called Magic.
