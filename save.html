This is for those end-of-the-month, deadline-filled, I-only-have-an-hour-til-my-next-lecture meals!

Chilli Daddy

Chilli Daddy is a chinese restaurant selling sze-chuan style food. Spice levels go from levels 0-5 so love it or hate it, there is enough sze-chuan food for everyone!

Mission Burrito

Mission Burrito sells cheap, student-friendly burritos in the middle of Park Street! Vegan to carnivorous, they've got it all. And, if you grab a loyalty card, you'll be scoffing your 10th burrito for free.

Piri-Piri

Piri-piri is halal and delicious! Need a quick, tasty meal? Come here and kill two birds with one stone.

Fresh Takeaway

Fresh is famously known for feeding High Kingsdown residents and those who frequent the ASS (Arts and Social Sciences) library.

Wok to Walk

Wok to Walk is located at the bottom of Park Street and is the simplest chinese takeaway place around - plus it boasts a 20% student discount before 11pm!

Beerd

Don't be discouraged by the hipster decor and the high quality food - Beerd's lunchtime deals are very budget-friendly. If you're feeling cheeky, you can also nab yourself a craft beer!

Eat a Pitta

Located right opposite Wills Memorial Building, it offers falafel for just £2.50. You don't have to venture far from your books to indulge in a healthy, filling and (optionally) vegan meal.

Dragon Fountain

Yet more proof that delicious food doesn't have to break the bank! Dragon Fountain's 30 year heritage of great-quality takeout means that your late-night cravings will always be well-satisfied.

Vincenzo's Pizza House

Conveniently located on Park Street and with extremely generous portion sizes for their pizza and pasta dishes, Vinceno's brings a slice of Italy to Bristol.

Mezze Palace

You will find few places with as much value for money - £5 lunches in a cozy city centre restaurant? You might be wondering what the catch is, but we can't find one! It's safe to say that at Mezze Palace, you'll eat like royalty.

Diamond Kebab

Halal, open at inordinate times of night and a reliable source of cheesy chips. Kebabs are the de facto national food and Diamond Kebab knows how to fly the flag.

Taka Taka

Taka Taka is the restaurant equivalent of your most reliable, cheerful, kind friend. They are there for you at all hours and they're always happy to snap you up a quick meal when you're a little tipsy and need something to ground you. You visit this friend and you always leave feeling better. It's not a mistake that their rolls are called Magic.

Rendezvous

Look, this was the best photo we could find. But trust us when we say that you will not be disappointed with Rendezvous. It's a cheap and cheerful chippy that fills you up, it gives you a great Fri-yay night treat. Plus, it might just be where you have your most important Rendezvous!

Tiffins Bristol

Gluten free, vegan, beautifully spiced, amazing owners and right next to the ASS Library? This is Bristol's greatest treasure. Pro tip: Tiffins has been known to employ hungry students - if you want food AND cash, make yourself known.
